CLASSIC ICED COFFEE WITH MILK

A creamy, delicious classic iced coffee

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup brewed coffee (cooled)
  • 1/2 cup milk or cream
  • Ice cubes
  • 1â€“2 teaspoons sugar (optional)

Instruction:

  1. Brew the Coffee
    Brew a cup of coffee and let it cool.
  2. Sweeten (Optional)
    Stir in sugar if desired while the coffee is still warm.
  3. Serve
    Add ice cubes to a glass, pour in the cooled coffee, and top with milk or cream.
  4. Stir & Enjoy Stir to combine, and enjoy your classic creamy iced coffee.
