CLASSIC HOT COFFEE

Recipe Information

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (240 ml) water
  • 1 to 2 tablespoons ground coffee (depending on how strong you like it)
  • Milk or cream (optional)
  • Sugar or sweetener (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil Water
    Heat the water in a kettle or pot until just boiling.
  2. Brew Coffee
    • Use a French press, drip coffee maker, pour-over, or any brewing method you prefer.
    • For French press: Add ground coffee, pour hot water, stir, let steep for 4 minutes, then press down slowly.
    • For pour-over: Place a filter in the dripper, add coffee grounds, pour hot water slowly in a circular motion.
  3. Add Extras (Optional)
    • Add milk, cream, or plant-based milk if desired.
    • Sweeten with sugar, honey, or your favorite sweetener.
  4. Serve
    Pour into your favorite mug and enjoy hot.
