About Coffee-

Description

Coffee is a popular brewed beverage made from the roasted and ground seeds (beans) of the Coffea genus, characterized by its dark color, bitter taste, and acidity, primarily due to its caffeine content which provides a stimulating effect.

Originating in Ethiopia, the process involves harvesting coffee berries, drying and roasting their seeds into beans, and then brewing the grounds in hot water. Two main species, Arabica and Robusta, are commercially cultivated, with Arabica generally prized for its fine flavor and Robusta for its higher caffeine content.
